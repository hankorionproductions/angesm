Hand Anthropometry Research

Co-Author

Research was conducted to complete my undergraduate study in Industrial Engineering Department at Gadjah Mada University. An anthropometric survey was done by measuring 24 hand dimensions of 202 Indonesian young adult females. The results of this study can be utilized as a consideration in the design or selection of the hand-operated products that are utilized by Indonesian young adult females.
